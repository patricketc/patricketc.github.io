Meyers 200

Presentation for EAA AirVenture Oshkosh

Aircraft Overview

This Meyers 200 has been meticulously restored and maintained. It is a high‑performance, all‑metal, four‑seat aircraft built with extraordinary craftsmanship.

  • Model Year: 1965
  • Engine: Continental IO‑520
  • Max Cruise: 200 mph
  • Notable Upgrades: Modern avionics, fresh interior

Restoration Details

The airframe has been stripped, inspected, and refinished in a custom paint scheme. Interior updated with new leather seats and soundproofing. Avionics upgraded to a full glass panel.

Special Features

This Meyers 200 is one of only 135 built and is recognized for its strength and speed. The aircraft has a unique history including:

  • Original logbooks with complete maintenance history
  • Rare factory options preserved
  • Active participation in cross‑country air races
